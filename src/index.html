My Craft in a Nutshell

I believe simplicity is undervalued and therefore strive to make things as simple as possible.
I am also very concerned about best practices and always sacrifice developer experience over user experience.

Front-end

I value simple content structure, clean design patterns, and thoughtful interactions filled with semantics and performance. I tend to code things from scratch and enjoy bringing ideas to life.

Back-end
